Kimchi

Description

Ingredients

  • Napa Cabbage
  • Gochugaru
  • Honey/Corn Syrup
  • Salt
  • Salted Shrimp
  • Garlic
  • Ginger

Steps

  1. Chop the cabbage and toss generously in salt. Let sit for on hour in a strainer over a bowl.
  2. In a small bowl add gochugaru, minced garlic, minced salted shrimp, grated ginger, and honey. Adjust for taste.
  3. Rinse excess salt off kimchi. Rinse at least 2 times and shake off excess water.
  4. In a large bowl mix kimchi well with the sauce.
  5. If desired, you may add shredded radish, carrot and green onions.
  6. Transfer into a clean container.
  7. Can sit at room temperature for one day before refrigeration is required.
