Thit Kho

Description

Ingredients

  • Fish Sauce
  • Burnt Sugar Syrup
  • Pork Belly
  • Garlic
  • Hard-boiled Eggs(Optional)
  • Coconut Water

Steps

  1. Slice and parboil the pork belly. Rinse and just cover pork with coconut water.
  2. Smash a few garlic cloves and toss into pot with pork.
  3. Add 1/4 cup of fish sauce, 4 tbsp of burnt sugar syrup.
  4. Simmer until tender. adding water to keep pork submerged.
  5. Add peeled boiled eggs in if you wish.
  6. Serve over rice.
