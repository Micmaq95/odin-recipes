Pho

Description

Ingredients

  • Spices
  • Beef Neck Bones
  • Beef Shank
  • Pho Noodles
  • Herbs

Steps

  1. In a large stock pot, parboil the beef bones and shank. Rinse and bring to boil again in new water.
  2. Toast the spices. Add to a spice bag and then into the pot.
  3. Simmer until shank is tender. Remove to chill for easier slicing.
  4. Season broth to taste.
  5. Blanch pho noodles in a separate saucepan.
  6. Assemble bowl with blanched noodles, herbs and sliced beef shank.
  7. Pour broth into bowl and serve.
